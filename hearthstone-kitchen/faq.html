Frequently asked questions

ShopAboutFAQ

How long does delivery take?

Standard UK delivery is 7-14 working days. Tracking is provided via email once your supplier dispatches.

What's your return policy?

You have 14 days from receipt under UK Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013. Email us with your order number and we'll send return instructions.

Is checkout secure?

Yes. All payments are processed by Stripe — we never see or store your card details. The full checkout happens on Stripe's servers, not ours.

Do you ship outside the UK?

We currently ship to UK and Republic of Ireland. International delivery is on our roadmap.

How do I track my order?

Once the supplier dispatches, you'll receive a tracking link via email within 3-5 working days of your order.

What if my item arrives damaged?

Email us within 30 days at the address on your receipt. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost.

Are your items genuine?

Yes. Every item is sourced from supplier partners with verifiable inventory. No counterfeits, no grey market.
