# Skyscraper Shops

The public-facing storefronts of Skyscraper — a family of fifteen UK online shops.

This repo holds only the deployed static sites. Connected to Netlify for auto-deploy on push to main.

## Live
- https://skyscraperhq.uk (master landing — pending DNS)
- 15 shop subpaths under master

## Shops
Lumière Beauty · Pinwheel Toys · Pawsworth · Hearthstone Kitchen · Vector Tech · Trailhead Outdoor · Stretch Fitness · Quilltree Office · Voyage & Co · Greenhouse Lane · Little Robin · Inkwell & Co · Twilight Wellness · Living Light Eco · Maker Lane

## Tech
Static HTML/CSS/JS · Stripe Checkout · Cloudflare Email Routing
