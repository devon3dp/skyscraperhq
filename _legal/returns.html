Returns & Refunds

Last updated: 2026-06-11

14-day right to cancel

Under UK law you have 14 days from receipt of your order to cancel and request a refund, no reason needed.

How to return

  1. Email us at knechtelross@gmail.com with your order number and reason.
  2. We'll provide return instructions and address.
  3. Send the item back (you cover return shipping unless item was faulty).
  4. Once received, we refund within 14 days to your original payment method.

Faulty or damaged items

If your item arrives faulty or damaged, email us within 30 days. We'll replace or refund at no cost.

Items excluded

For hygiene reasons, opened beauty items, used baby teething products, and personalised items cannot be returned unless faulty.
